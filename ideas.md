  </core_principles>
  <color_philosophy>
    **"Midnight & Neon"**:
    - Background: Rich Black/Deep Navy (Infinite depth)
    - Text: Silver/Cool Grey (Modernity)
    - Accent: Electric Blue or Emerald Green (Subtle glow for active elements)
    - *Reasoning*: Suggests a 24/7 service that operates in the background of your life, always ready.
  </color_philosophy>
  <layout_paradigm>
    **Split-Screen & Cards**:
    - Vertical split screens for desktop (Image | Content).
    - Glassmorphism cards for services, floating above the dark background.
  </layout_paradigm>
  <signature_elements>
    1. **Glassmorphism**: Frosted glass effects for navigation and cards.
    2. **Glow Effects**: Subtle ambient light behind key elements.
    3. **Vertical Navigation**: A side navigation bar instead of top, mimicking a dashboard or app.
  </signature_elements>
  <interaction_philosophy>
    **"Fluid Response"**:
    - Elements react magnetically to the cursor.
    - Buttons have a "glow" on hover.
  </interaction_philosophy>

    - **Smooth Scroll**: Custom scroll damping for a premium feel.
